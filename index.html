Introduction

About AI Latin America Institute

Fostering the ethical development and application of AI across Latin America and U.S. Hispanic communities

The AI Latin America Institute (AILA) is a cross-continental initiative dedicated to catalyzing economic and social development through Artificial Intelligence (AI). We focus on advancing innovation, education, and responsible governance across Latin America and among Hispanic and Latino communities in the United States. By leveraging AI to address key challenges such as poverty, inequality, education, and access to opportunities in the digital economy, AILA serves as a bridge between cultures and economies.

Our Vision

To become the leading institution promoting AI-driven innovation and sustainable development across Latin America and among Hispanic and Latino communities in the United States, focusing on ethical governance, education, and inclusive growth.

Our Mission

To create a regional center for education, research, and policy development that empowers Latin American countries and U.S. Hispanic and Latino communities to harness AI for societal benefit. AILA fosters a collaborative environment where governments, industries, and academic institutions work together to develop AI solutions aligned with ethical principles and regional challenges.
